Senior Everything Engineer

Learning a new language or framework is all in a day's work for you. You know all the engineering best practices from BDD to Continuous Deployment but are not bound by them. You thrive on pushing the envelope and challenging yourself and your colleagues. You are active in the open source community and can be found at a whiteboard presenting new technologies to your fellow techies.

At Lucid Technics, you will get to use the best technologies for the job whether they are well known or created by you on the fly. You may lead a team or be part of a super team of like minded engineers. As part of Lucid Technics, you will create scalable software for our clients and our products.

Junior Everything Engineer

You are a developer who gets it. Even though you are just starting out you have contributed to open source projects or have your own pet projects. You are always learning and can pick up languages and frameworks quick.

At Lucid Technics, you will be asked to do everything from setting up continuous integration servers to writing scalable applications using Node.js and cloud computing. You will get work on interesting projects and contribute as part of a team.

Web UI/UX Designer

You understand the nuances of User Experience Design and are constantly refining your techniques. You will work on crafting interfaces for both Lucid Technics' clients and products.
