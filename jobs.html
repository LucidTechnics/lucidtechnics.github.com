Internships - Technology

You are just starting out and are looking for a chance to experience developing interesting stuff you would be proud to put on your resume. You know, contributing to cool open source coding projects that you can proudly point to on Github? Join us for one summer and you will be able to prove to the world you were exposed to ... the good and bad of functional programming; hacking JavaScript on big data; rapid application development on major cloud computing platforms; and don't forget nifty natural language processing, machine learning, and code generation techniques.

At Lucid Technics, you will be encouraged to explore our list of open projects incubating in our open source lab. We will task you with understanding how things work now and challenge you to come up with cool ways to make them work better. And all this in a cool working environment that will expose you to rock star developers based in Washington DC, Europe, and Asia.

Senior Software Engineer

Learning a new language or framework is all in a day's work for you. You thrive on pushing the envelope and challenging yourself and your colleagues. You are active in the open source community and can be found at a whiteboard presenting new ideas to your fellow techies.

At Lucid Technics, you may lead a team or be part of a super team of like minded engineers. As part of Lucid Technics, you will create scalable software using Airlift and cloud computing for our clients.

Junior Software Engineer

You are a developer who gets it. Even though you are just starting out you have contributed to open source projects or have your own pet projects. You are always learning and can pick up languages and frameworks quickly.

At Lucid Technics, you will learn how to write scalable applications using Airlift and cloud computing. You will get to work on interesting projects that involve machine learning and natural language processing and you will feel rewarded as you contribute to our team.

Web UI/UX Designer

You understand the nuances of User Experience Design and are constantly refining your techniques. You will work on crafting beautiful and functional interfaces for Lucid Technics' clients as well as for our products.

At Lucid Technics, you will get to apply your unique interface ideas to improve our client's projects. You may lead a team or be part of a super team of like minded engineers.

Data Scientist - Statistician

You are excited to actually put all of that math you learned in school to good use. You are familiar with R or Octave and can't wait to get your hands on our clients' data. You also dabble in programming and are not afraid to write a quick script in Python or JavaScript just to get stuff done.

At Lucid Technics, you will be encouraged to find better uses for our clients' data. Our clients will thank you for making their business more successful as you identify possibilities to increase revenues and reduce costs.
